PROJECT TITLE: Programming Assignment 2
PURPOSE OF PROJECT: Demonstrate understanding of decision structures in C++.
VERSION or DATE: 9-29-2014

The program asks the user the following questions:
	1. Do they reside in Mecklenburg or Cabarrus county.
	2. The price of the house they wish to buy.
	3. The down payment they wish to place.
	4. Their annual income.
	5. Their monthly expenses.
After this, the program decides whether or not the user qualifies for a home loan.
It also displays the users debt-to-income ratio.
